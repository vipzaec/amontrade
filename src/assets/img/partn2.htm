EcoFlow Technology

EcoFlow aims to empower people and communities through portable, clean, and reliable power for lasting impact. Founded in 2016, EcoFlow focuses product development on performance, safety, and convenience, harnessing powerful and new technologies to revolutionize the way people everywhere access and interact with power solutions. RIVER Portable Power Station is the first of several clean, mobile, sustainable power products in development. Learn more about EcoFlow here.

The Solar Foundation

The Solar Foundation is an independent 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization whose mission is to accelerate adoption of the world’s most abundant energy source. Through our leadership, research, and capacity building, we create transformative solutions to achieve a prosperous future in which solar and solar-compatible technologies are integrated into all aspects of our lives. Solar Saves Lives is an initiative led by The Solar Foundation to bring solar and storage technologies to Puerto Rico, in partnership with major humanitarian relief organizations.
